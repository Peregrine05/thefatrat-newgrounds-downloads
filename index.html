ⓘ Important Information (README)

Use Ctrl+F to search for a song in the list.

The main download links are preferred. Using many Alternative download links in a short time period may result in you being rate-limited. The main download links may or may not result in the same.

Your browser may open and play the songs directly instead of downloading them. To actually download the song, use Ctrl+S on the tab that is playing the song, or right-click on the download link and then click Save link as, or similar.

The main download links are directly to the internal audio files used by Newgrounds. The Alternative download links to the download button of the webpage, if it exists.
